About Us

Traffic sign detection is an important task in the field of computer vision, specifically in the context of autonomous driving, driver assistance systems, and intelligent transportation systems. The goal is to automatically detect and recognize traffic signs in real-time from images or video streams captured by cameras mounted on vehicles or at roadside locations.
